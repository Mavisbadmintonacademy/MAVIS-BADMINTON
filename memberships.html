Common Queries

Membership FAQ

What are the student coaching timings and batches?

We run two daily coaching batches for students: Batch 1 from 5:30 PM to 7:00 PM, and Batch 2 from 6:30 PM to 8:00 PM (Monday to Friday). A dedicated Weekend Batch (Saturday & Sunday) is also available at ₹2,000/month.

Do I need to pay a security deposit?

A refundable security deposit of ₹2,000 is only required for our standard Monthly Pass. The 6-Month, 10-Month, and Hourly Casual Play plans are completely deposit-free.

Are coaching charges included in the memberships?

Our standard passes cover court access and membership privileges. Professional coaching programs and dedicated training batches are available separately, or can be bundled. Get in touch with our coaches for detailed training curriculums.

How do I reserve slots with my membership?

Members get access to our priority booking calendar. You can select your preferred shift (Morning/Evening) and reserve your court time slot. Slots are booked in 1-hour increments.

Can I rent playing equipment at the facility?

Yes! We offer premium quality badminton carbon-fiber racquets and professional feather/nylon shuttlecocks for rent at the academy reception desk at highly affordable hourly rates.
